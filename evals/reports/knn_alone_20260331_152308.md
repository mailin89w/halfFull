## Caveats

> **KNN coverage gaps** ‚Äî The following conditions have no KNN lab-group support and will never be flagged by the KNN layer alone.  Their recall is structurally 0%, and they are excluded from top-1 / top-3 denominator calculations: `electrolyte_imbalance`, `hypothyroidism`, `perimenopause`, `sleep_disorder`, `vitamin_d_deficiency`.

> **Score interpretation** ‚Äî KNN scores are raw lift-sum values (not probabilities). A score of 4.0 means the two supporting lab groups each had a neighbourhood lift of ~2.0√ó, just above the MIN_LIFT=2.0 gate.  Higher scores indicate stronger neighbourhood consensus.  Do not compare magnitudes directly to ML model probabilities in the layer1 report.

> **Anemia vs iron_deficiency** ‚Äî Both conditions share the same lab groups (`cbc`, `iron_studies`).  KNN cannot distinguish between them from lab signals alone; they will always receive identical scores.

> **Hepatitis vs liver** ‚Äî Both conditions rely solely on `liver_panel` signals. Same disambiguation limitation applies.
